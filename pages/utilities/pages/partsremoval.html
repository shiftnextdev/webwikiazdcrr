Remove Mobility parts out of Custody

Parts like BBU's sometimes find their way into the custody of Rack Receiving personel (usually when bbu problems happen in boost). When this happens they can not be used in racks where they are needed. Its best to clear any of these out, and can be done using Mobility. This video goes through the process of checking custody in Mobility for any parts that may appear and removing them using the removal tools also in Mobility
