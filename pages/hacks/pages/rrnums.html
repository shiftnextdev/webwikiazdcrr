RACK RECEIVING QUICK COUNTS

MATERIAL QUANTITIES & TIME PREDICTIONS

01

TRUCK/RACK QUANTITY LIMITS

  • 1 truck  =  14 racks

  • 2 truck  =  28 racks

  • 3 truck  =  42 racks

02

COMMON ELAPSED RECEIVE TIME

RACKS OVER TRUCK LIMIT REQUIRES ADDITIONAL TRUCK

  • 14 racks  =  2hrs

  • 28 racks  =  4hrs

  • 42 racks  =  6hrs

    • *** 2hrs Rack Receiving/truck ***

  • 22 racks  =  1 truck + additional = 4hrs

  • 30 racks  =  2 truck + additional = 6hrs

  • 48 racks  =  3 truck + additional = 8hrs

03

MATERIAL PREP QUICK COUNTS

  • 14 racks  =  1 stack bbu  =  2 pallets (1 truck)

  • 28 racks  =  4 stack bbu  =  8 pallets (2 truck)

  • 42 racks  =  6 stack bbu  =  12 pallets (3 truck)

    • ***********BBUS ^************WHIPS v ************

  • 14 racks  =  1/2 bin whips  = 1.5 pallet whips (1 truck)

  • 28 racks  =  1 bin whips  =  3 pallet whips (2 truck)

  • 42 racks  =  3/2 bin whips  =  4.5 pallet whips (3 truck)

04

INVENTORY REQUIREMENTS

  • 1 truck = 04 pallets bbu | 1.5 pallet whips

  • 2 truck = 08 pallets bbu | 3.0 pallet whips

  • 3 truck = 12 pallets bbu | 4.5 pallet whips

*** Always have 1 truck worth of inventory prepped (2 stacks bbu, 0.5 bin whips) on standby ***

05

RULE OF 2

2X(#TRUCKS)

  • 1 truck 2 stacks of bbu, 2hrs

  • 2 truck 4 stacks of bbu, 4hrs

  • 3 truck 6 stacks of bbu, 6hrs

06

SUMMARY MATERIALS READY

  • 14 racks = 1 truck | 0.5 bin whips | 1.5 pallet whips | 2 stacks bbu | 04 pallets bbu | 2hrs

  • 28 racks = 2 truck | 1.0 bin whips | 3.0 pallet whips | 4 stacks bbu | 08 pallets bbu | 4hrs

  • 42 racks = 3 truck | 1.5 bin whips | 4.5 pallet whips | 6 stacks bbu | 12 pallets bbu | 6hrs

07

ACCOUNTING BREIF

ANY RACKS OVER TRUCK QTY LIMIT +2HRS RR TIME

  • 14 racks/truck

  • 4.0 pallets bbu/truck

  • 1.5 pallets of whips/truck

  • 2hrs Rack Receiving/truck
